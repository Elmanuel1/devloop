- For invoice documents, partial payments are acceptable if indicated.

4. TOTAL AMOUNT CONSISTENCY (Weight: 15%)

| Document Type    | Amount Comparison                          | Score |
|-----------------|-------------------------------------------|-------|
| delivery_slip    | Amount < PO total (subset)                | 0.8–1.0 (normal) |

   - If available and matches: boost confidence by 10%
   - If different: reduce confidence by 15%

6. PO NUMBER MATCH (Bonus factor)
   - If PO number in extracted doc matches candidate displayId → HIGH confidence boost

- **HIGH CONFIDENCE (>0.85)**: Vendor exact/very similar AND date within 2 weeks (or delivery date after order date) AND most/all extracted items found in PO.  
- **MEDIUM CONFIDENCE (0.60-0.85)**: Vendor similar AND date within 1 month AND some extracted items match PO items.  
- **LOW CONFIDENCE (<0.60)**: Vendor different OR extracted items not in PO OR date too far apart (>2 months) OR extracted amount > PO total (unless document indicates partial payment/shipment).
